## Key facts
Public transport options are available from Incheon Airport late at night, but they are limited compared to daytime services. **Airport buses** and **night buses** operate during late-night hours, while **AREX** trains have limited schedules.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Fastest option | Taxi (24/7) |
| Cheapest option | Night buses (₩17,000–₩18,000) |
| Approx. time | Night buses: ~120 min frequency |
| Official source | [seoul-airport.com](https://seoul-airport.com/arex.php) |

## Fastest option
Taxis are the fastest option from Incheon Airport late at night. They are available 24/7, providing a convenient and direct route to your destination. While more expensive than public transport, taxis are reliable when other services have ended.

## Cheapest option
Night buses are the cheapest late-night public transport option from Incheon Airport, with fares generally ranging between **₩17,000–₩18,000**. They provide essential links to downtown Seoul when other services have stopped.

## Best for hotels
For travelers heading to hotels, airport limousine buses offer a comfortable ride with convenient stops at major hotels and districts in Seoul. However, check the specific route schedules as they may vary.

## Best late at night
Night buses are the best public transport option late at night, with routes like **N6000** and **N6701** serving key areas in Seoul. They operate at intervals of approximately **120 minutes**.

## Step-by-step
1. Determine your arrival time at Incheon Airport.
2. Check the availability of night buses or airport limousine buses for your destination.
3. Purchase a ticket at the airport or via a mobile app if available.
4. Follow signs to the bus terminal or taxi stand.

> Tip: Always verify the latest schedules online or at the airport to ensure you have the most current information.

## Good to know
- **AREX trains** are not available late at night, so plan accordingly if arriving after 11:00 PM.
- **Night buses** provide a cost-effective option, but expect longer travel times due to less frequent service.
