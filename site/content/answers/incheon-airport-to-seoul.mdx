## Key facts
To travel from Incheon Airport to Seoul, you can choose from several options including the AREX train, limousine bus, taxi, and private transfers. Each option varies in travel time and cost.

| Option | Time | Approx. price | Best for | Pros | Watch out |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| AREX Express | ~43 min | ₩9,500–11,000 | Fast travel | Direct to Seoul Station | None |
| AREX All-Stop | ~60 min | ₩4,150–4,950 | Budget travel | Multiple stops | Longer duration |
| Limousine Bus | 60-90 min | ₩15,000–18,000 | Comfort | Ample luggage space | Traffic delays |
| Taxi | ~60 min | ₩65,000–100,000 | Door-to-door | Convenient | Expensive |
| Private Transfer | ~60 min | ₩90,000–160,000 | Personalized service | Direct and private | Costly |

## Fastest option
The **AREX Express Train** is the fastest option, taking approximately 43 minutes to reach Seoul Station directly without any stops. This option is ideal for those prioritizing speed and efficiency.

## Cheapest option
The **AREX All-Stop Train** is the most economical choice, costing between ₩4,150 and ₩4,950. It takes about 60 minutes, stopping at various stations along the way.

## Best for hotels
If you are heading to a hotel, the **Limousine Bus** might be the best choice. It offers routes to various neighborhoods in Seoul with comfortable seating and ample luggage space, though travel time can vary due to traffic.

## Best late at night
For late-night arrivals, **Taxis** or **Private Transfers** are the most reliable options. Taxis are readily available outside the arrivals area, providing direct service to your destination.

## Step-by-step
- **AREX Train**: Follow signs to the AREX station in the basement of the terminal. Purchase a ticket at the counter or use a T-money card.
- **Limousine Bus**: Locate the ticket counter on the ground floor and check the platform for your specific route.
- **Taxi**: Head to the taxi stand outside the arrivals hall.
- **Private Transfer**: Pre-book a service and meet your driver at the designated pick-up point.

> Tip: Always check the current schedule and fares on the official websites or at the airport to ensure the most accurate information.

## Good to know
- **Tickets**: Ensure you have a valid ticket or transportation card like T-money for trains and buses.
- **Luggage**: Consider the luggage capacity if using buses.
- **Traffic**: Travel times can vary significantly with traffic, especially for buses and taxis.
