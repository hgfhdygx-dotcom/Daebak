## Short answer

To get from Incheon International Airport (ICN) to central Seoul, the three common choices are the AREX airport railroad, an airport limousine bus, or a taxi. Most first-time visitors heading toward Seoul Station or a subway-connected hotel take the AREX train because it avoids road traffic.

## The AREX airport railroad

The AREX (Airport Railroad Express) runs directly beneath both passenger terminals, so you do not need to leave the airport to board. There are two services on the same line: the **Express** train, which runs nonstop to Seoul Station in about 43 minutes, and the **all-stop commuter** train, which is cheaper, stops at several stations along the way, and takes roughly an hour. At Seoul Station you can transfer to the subway, KTX high-speed rail, or a taxi.

| Option | Route | Approx. time | Best for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| AREX Express | Nonstop to Seoul Station | ~43 min | Speed, luggage, Seoul Station hotels |
| AREX all-stop | Stops en route, subway transfers | ~60 min | Lower fare, subway-connected stays |
| Limousine bus | Direct to neighborhoods/hotels | Varies with traffic | Hotels far from a station |
| Taxi | Door to door | Varies with traffic | Late arrivals, heavy luggage, groups |

## Airport limousine buses

Airport limousine buses depart from clearly numbered stops on the arrivals level and run to many Seoul districts and major hotels. A bus can be the most convenient choice when your accommodation is not near a subway station, because it drops you closer to your door. Travel time depends on road traffic, so allow extra time during rush hour.

## Taxis and late arrivals

Taxis are available 24 hours a day at the airport, which makes them useful if you land after the trains and buses stop running. Fares are metered and vary with distance and traffic, and some routes include expressway tolls. For a late-night arrival with luggage, a taxi is often the simplest option even though it usually costs more than the train.

## Which should you choose?

If you are staying near Seoul Station or a subway line and want a predictable arrival time, the AREX train is usually the best balance of speed and cost. If your hotel is hard to reach by subway, compare a limousine bus against a taxi based on your arrival time and how much luggage you are carrying.
