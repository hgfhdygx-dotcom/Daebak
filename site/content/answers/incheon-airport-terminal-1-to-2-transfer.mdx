## When you need it
Transferring between Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 at Incheon International Airport is necessary for passengers with connecting flights or those arriving at one terminal and departing from another. The terminals are approximately **6 kilometers apart**.

## How to use it
The primary transportation option for transferring between Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 is the **free shuttle bus**. This service operates regularly between the two terminals, ensuring a convenient transfer.

- **Shuttle Bus**: Operates every 5-10 minutes.
- **Location**: Bus stops are clearly marked outside the arrivals area of each terminal.

> Tip: Always check the latest schedule on the official airport website or information desks upon arrival.

## Cost
The shuttle bus between Terminal 1 and Terminal 2 is **free of charge**. This service is provided by the airport to facilitate easy and cost-effective transfers for all passengers.

> Good to know: The shuttle bus is the most reliable and cost-effective method for transferring between terminals at Incheon Airport.
