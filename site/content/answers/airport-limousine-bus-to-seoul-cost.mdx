## What is the current fare for the airport limousine bus from Incheon International Airport to various locations in Seoul?
The **standard fare** for the airport limousine bus from Incheon International Airport to Seoul city center typically ranges between **₩17,000 and ₩18,000** per adult. Some routes may offer fares as low as **₩10,000**, but these are less common.

## Are there different prices for different bus routes or destinations within Seoul?
Yes, prices vary slightly depending on the route, operator, and service class. For example, Route 6001 to Seoul Station is **₩17,000**, while Route 6002 to Gangnam is **₩18,000**. **Premium routes** may cost more but generally fall within the same range.

## What is the payment method accepted for purchasing tickets for the airport limousine bus?
Tickets for the airport limousine bus can be purchased using **cash or credit/debit cards**. It's advisable to check with the specific bus operator for any additional payment options.

> Good to know: **Most routes charge ₩17,000–₩18,000**, so plan your budget accordingly.
