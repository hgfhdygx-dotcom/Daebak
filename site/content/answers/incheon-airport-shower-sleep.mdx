## When you need it

Incheon Airport provides both shower and sleeping facilities for travelers. **Free showers** are available airside in Terminal 1 near Gates 23 and 28, accessible 24 hours for transit passengers. For a more private experience, paid options are available in lounges and landside facilities.

## How to use it

For showers, transit passengers can use the free facilities airside, while those who have cleared immigration can use landside options like Spa at Home or Darakhyu. Sleeping options include free nap zones with reclining chairs and paid services like the Darakhyu Capsule Hotel.

- **Free showers**: Bring your own towel and toiletries.
- **Paid showers**: Available in lounges and landside facilities with amenities included.
- **Sleeping**: Use nap zones for free or book a capsule hotel for more privacy.

> Tip: **Nap zones** can get crowded, especially after midnight, so plan accordingly.

## Cost

- **Free showers**: No cost, but bring personal toiletries.
- **Paid showers**: Approximately 7,000–15,000 KRW.
- **Capsule hotel**: Around 65,000 KRW for an overnight stay.

> Good to know: **Landside facilities** like Spa at Home offer comprehensive packages including towels and toiletries for around 10,000–11,000 KRW.
