| Domestic flights | Arrive 1.5-2 hours early |

| Peak hour wait | Up to 1 hour |

## When you need it
For international flights from Incheon Airport, you should **arrive at least 3 hours** before your scheduled departure. This time frame allows for check-in, security screening, and immigration procedures. For domestic flights, arriving **1.5 to 2 hours** early is generally sufficient.

## How to use it
Upon arriving at Incheon Airport, proceed to check-in, then head to security screening. Security wait times average **10-20 minutes**, but can extend to **45 minutes or more** during peak hours, especially at Terminal 2. Use real-time wait time tools available at the airport to choose less crowded entrances.

> Tip: Consider using self check-in or mobile check-in to save time, especially if you have only carry-on luggage.

## Cost
There is no direct cost associated with arriving early at Incheon Airport. However, being early ensures that you have ample time to handle any unforeseen delays or long queues, especially during peak travel times.

> Good to know: During peak hours, especially in Terminal 2, security wait times can exceed **45 minutes**, so plan accordingly.
