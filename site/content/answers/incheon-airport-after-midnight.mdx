## When you need it
If you land at Incheon International Airport after midnight, consider using night buses, taxis, or staying at airport hotels. **Night buses and taxis** are the primary transportation options available after midnight.

- **Night Buses:** Operate from around 23:40 to 04:50, with routes covering major Seoul districts like Myeongdong and Gangnam. 
- **Taxis:** Available 24/7, though a late-night surcharge applies. 
- **Airport Hotels:** Capsule hotels and transit hotels are open for overnight stays.

> Tip: Check your exact arrival time against the last bus and train schedules to plan effectively.

## How to use it
Upon landing, proceed through customs and immigration, which typically takes 30 to 60 minutes. Then choose your transportation or accommodation option:

- **Night Buses:** Purchase tickets at kiosks near bus stops at Terminal 1 or 2.
- **Taxis:** Follow signs to the taxi stands outside the terminal.
- **Capsule Hotels:** Located landside in both terminals, book in advance if possible.

> Good to know: Currency exchange services are available 24/7 if you need local currency.

## Cost
The costs for transportation and accommodation vary:

- **Night Buses:** ₩17,000–₩18,000 per person.
- **Taxis:** ₩85,000–₩130,000, including late-night surcharge.
- **Capsule Hotels:** Approximately ₩62,000 for a single overnight stay.

> Tip: Consider sharing a taxi if traveling in a group to reduce costs.
