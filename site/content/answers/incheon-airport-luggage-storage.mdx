## When you need it
Luggage storage at Incheon Airport is available for travelers needing short or long-term storage. **Hanjin Express** offers services in both Terminal 1 and Terminal 2, making it convenient for all travelers.

## How to use it
To store luggage, visit the **Hanjin Express counters** located in both terminals. In Terminal 1, counters are near Gates 2 and 13, and in Terminal 2, a 24-hour service is available on the 3rd floor opposite Check-in H.

- **Terminal 1**: 1F east side (Gate 2), 1F west side (Gate 13), 3F near Check-in B/N
- **Terminal 2**: 3F opposite Check-in H (24-hour), 1F near Arrival Gate 1

> Tip: For 24-hour access, use the Terminal 2 service on the 3rd floor.

## Cost
Luggage storage fees vary by size and duration. **Staffed counters** charge between ₩4,000 and ₩18,000 per day, depending on the size of the luggage. **Self-service lockers** are also available for short-term storage, with fees starting at ₩2,700 for small items.

- **Small bag**: ₩4,000–₩6,000 per day
- **Medium suitcase**: ₩6,000–₩9,000 per day
- **Large suitcase**: ₩8,000–₩12,000 per day
- **Extra-large items**: ₩10,000–₩18,000 per day

> Good to know: Self-service lockers are best for quick, flexible storage, while staffed options may offer better handling for larger or valuable items.
