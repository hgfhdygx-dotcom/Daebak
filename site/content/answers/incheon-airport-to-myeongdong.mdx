## Key facts
To travel from Incheon Airport to Myeongdong, you have three main options: train, bus, and taxi. Each varies in cost and travel time, so choose based on your budget and convenience.

| Option | Time | Approx. price | Best for | Pros | Watch out |
|--------|------|---------------|----------|------|-----------|
| AREX Express + Subway | ~60 min | ₩9,500–11,000 | Fast and affordable | Quick transfer | Requires a subway transfer |
| Airport Limousine Bus | ~60-90 min | ₩15,000–18,000 | Comfort with luggage | Direct to Myeongdong | Slower in traffic |
| Taxi | ~60 min | ₩65,000–100,000 | Direct and convenient | Door-to-door service | Expensive |

## Fastest option
The **fastest option** from Incheon Airport to Myeongdong is taking the AREX Express train to Seoul Station, then transferring to Subway Line 4 to Myeongdong Station. This route takes about **60 minutes** in total.

## Cheapest option
For the **cheapest option**, use the AREX All-Stop train and transfer to the subway. This costs approximately **₩4,150–4,950** but takes longer due to multiple stops.

## Best for hotels
The **best option for those with hotels in Myeongdong** is the Airport Limousine Bus. It offers a direct route to Myeongdong and is ideal for travelers with heavy luggage.

## Best late at night
For late-night arrivals, **taxis** are the most reliable option. They are available 24/7, offering direct service to your destination, albeit at a higher cost.

## Step-by-step
1. **Train:** Take the AREX Express from Incheon Airport to Seoul Station (~43 min). Transfer to Subway Line 4 to reach Myeongdong Station (~17 min).
2. **Bus:** Board an Airport Limousine Bus directly to Myeongdong. Check the schedule for the next available bus.
3. **Taxi:** Follow signs to the taxi stand outside the arrivals hall and hire a taxi to Myeongdong.

> Tip: Use a T-money card for seamless travel on public transport.

## Good to know
- **Luggage:** Limousine buses have ample space for luggage.
- **Traffic:** Buses and taxis can be delayed by traffic, especially during peak hours.
- **Payment:** Taxis accept cash and cards; ensure you have enough for the fare.

> Good to know: Always confirm the latest schedules and prices on official websites or at the airport.
