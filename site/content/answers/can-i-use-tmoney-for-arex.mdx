## When you need it
T-money can be used on the AREX All-Stop train for travel between Incheon International Airport, Gimpo International Airport, and Seoul Station. **T-money is not accepted** on the AREX Express train, which requires a separate ticket purchase.

## How to use it
To use T-money on the AREX All-Stop train, simply tap your card at the entry and exit gates. Ensure your card has sufficient balance to cover the fare. For the Express train, purchase a ticket separately at the station or online.

> Good to know: Always check your T-money card balance before boarding to avoid any inconvenience.

## Cost
The fare for the AREX All-Stop train using T-money is approximately **₩4,750** for a one-way trip from Incheon Airport to Seoul Station. The Express train fare is about **₩13,000** and requires a separate ticket purchase.

> Tip: Consider using the All-Stop train with T-money for a cost-effective journey if time allows.
