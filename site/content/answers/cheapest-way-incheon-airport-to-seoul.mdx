## What is the cheapest way from Incheon Airport to Seoul?
The cheapest way from Incheon Airport to Seoul is the **AREX All Stop Train**, which costs around ₩4,150. This train stops at all stations between Incheon Airport and Seoul Station, taking approximately 60-70 minutes.

## Fastest option
The fastest option to travel from Incheon Airport to Seoul is the AREX Express Train. It takes about 43 minutes to reach Seoul Station without any stops.

## Cheapest option
The AREX All Stop Train is the most economical choice, with a fare of approximately ₩4,150. This option is ideal for budget-conscious travelers who do not mind the longer travel time of about 60-70 minutes.

## Best for hotels
For those staying at hotels in central Seoul, the AREX Express Train offers a quick and convenient ride directly to Seoul Station, minimizing the need for additional transfers.

## Best late at night
Taxis or ride-sharing services like KakaoTaxi are the best options late at night. They provide direct, door-to-door service, though they are more expensive than public transport.

## Step-by-step
1. **Purchase a T-Money card**: Available at the airport for convenient travel on public transport.
2. **Locate the AREX station**: Follow signs to the basement level of the terminal.
3. **Board the AREX All Stop Train**: Enjoy the ride to Seoul Station, stopping at various stations along the way.

## Good to know
> **Tip:** Consider purchasing a T-Money card at the airport for discounts on public transportation, including the AREX train and buses.
