## When you need it
AREX tickets are essential for traveling from Incheon Airport to Seoul via the Airport Railroad Express. **Tickets can be purchased at various locations within the airport**, ensuring easy access to the train services.

## How to use it
To purchase AREX tickets at Incheon Airport, you have several options:
- **AREX Ticket Counters**: Located near the entrance to the AREX platforms in both Terminal 1 and Terminal 2.
- **Ticket Vending Machines**: Available at the entrance of the AREX stations, accepting cash and credit cards.
- **AREX Passenger Information Center**: Provides assistance and ticket sales.

> Good to know: Follow signs for the Airport Railroad (AREX) upon arrival to locate the ticketing areas easily.

## Cost
The cost of AREX tickets varies by service type:
- **Express Train (Non-stop)**: ₩13,000 for a one-way adult fare when purchased onsite; ₩16,000 for a round-trip.
- **All-Stop Train (Commuter)**: ₩4,750 for a one-way adult fare, with slight variations depending on the route.

**Booking Express tickets online** can save approximately ₩1,500 per one-way trip. Consider purchasing in advance for the best rates.
