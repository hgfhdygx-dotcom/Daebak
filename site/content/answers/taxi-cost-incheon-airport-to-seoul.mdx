## How much is a taxi from Incheon Airport to Seoul?
A taxi from Incheon Airport to Seoul costs between **65,000 and 100,000 KRW** for a standard taxi. The price varies based on your exact destination in Seoul and traffic conditions. Toll fees, which can range from 7,000 to 12,000 KRW, might be included or added separately.

## Price range for different taxi types
The cost of a taxi from Incheon Airport to Seoul varies by taxi type:

- **Standard Taxi**: 65,000–100,000 KRW
- **Deluxe Taxi**: 90,000–130,000 KRW
- **International Taxi**: 75,000–120,000 KRW

> Tip: Deluxe taxis offer a more comfortable ride and often have better English support.

## What affects the price?
The price of a taxi ride from Incheon Airport to Seoul can be influenced by several factors:

- **Destination**: Fares vary depending on the distance to specific areas within Seoul.
- **Traffic**: Heavy traffic can increase travel time and potentially the fare.
- **Time of Day**: Late-night surcharges of around 20% apply between midnight and 4 a.m.

## Cheaper alternatives
For those looking for cheaper alternatives, consider using the **Kakao T app** for ride-hailing, with fares estimated between 58,000 and 78,000 KRW. This service provides upfront cost estimates and operates from designated pick-up zones outside arrivals.

## Watch out
When taking a taxi from Incheon Airport, ensure you:

- Use **official taxi stands** located outside the arrivals hall at both Terminal 1 and Terminal 2.
- Avoid unofficial offers from touts inside the terminal.
- Have some Korean won on hand, as not all taxis may accept foreign cards despite most accepting credit cards and T-money.

> Good to know: Always confirm whether highway tolls are included in your fare to avoid unexpected charges.
