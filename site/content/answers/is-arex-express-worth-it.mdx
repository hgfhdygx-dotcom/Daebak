## The options at a glance
The AREX Express train offers a fast and convenient way to travel between Incheon International Airport and Seoul Station. Here’s how it compares to other options:

| Option | Time | Approx. price | Best for | Pros | Watch out |
|-------------------------|-------|---------------|----------------------|-------------------------------|---------------------------------|
| AREX Express | ~43 min | ₩9,500–11,000 | Speed and comfort | **Direct service, reserved seating** | Higher cost than all-stop train |
| AREX All-Stop | ~60 min | ₩4,150–4,950 | Budget travelers | Cheaper | Slower, multiple stops |
| Airport Limousine Bus | 60-90 min | ₩15,000–18,000 | Comfort, heavy luggage | Door-to-door service | Affected by traffic |
| Taxi | 60-90 min | ₩65,000–100,000 | Convenience, groups | Direct to destination | Expensive, traffic-dependent |

> **Tip:** For the fastest and most comfortable airport transfer, choose the AREX Express train.

## Best for each traveler
The AREX Express train is best for travelers prioritizing speed and comfort. It provides a direct, non-stop service to Seoul Station with reserved seating and ample luggage space, making it ideal after a long flight.

## Pros and cons
The AREX Express train's main advantages are its speed and comfort, thanks to direct service and reserved seating. However, it is more expensive than the all-stop train and may have less frequent departures.

## Which should you choose
If your priority is **speed and convenience**, the AREX Express train is worth the cost. For those on a tighter budget, the AREX All-Stop train offers a more economical alternative, albeit with a longer travel time.

> **Good to know:** The AREX Express train also offers free Wi-Fi, adding to its convenience for travelers needing to stay connected.
