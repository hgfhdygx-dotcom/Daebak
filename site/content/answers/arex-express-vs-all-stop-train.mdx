## The options at a glance
Traveling from Incheon International Airport to Seoul Station offers two main train options: the AREX Express and the all-stop train. Each has its own benefits depending on your priorities.

| Option | Time | Approx. price | Best for | Pros | Watch out |
|------------------|-------|---------------|------------------------|-------------------------------------|-------------------------|
| AREX Express | ~43 min | ₩9,500–11,000 | Fast travel to Seoul | **Non-stop**, reserved seating | Higher cost |
| All-stop Train | ~58 min | ₩4,150–4,950 | Budget travel | Cheaper, more stops | Longer travel time |

> **Tip:** If you're in a rush or prefer a more comfortable ride, the AREX Express is your best bet. For budget-conscious travelers or those with destinations along the route, the all-stop train is ideal.

## Best for each traveler
The AREX Express is best for travelers who prioritize speed and comfort, as it offers a non-stop journey with reserved seating. The all-stop train suits budget travelers or those whose destination is closer to one of the intermediate stops.

## Pros and cons
- **AREX Express**: **Pros** include a faster journey and more comfort. **Cons** are the higher ticket price.
- **All-stop Train**: **Pros** are the lower cost and more stops. **Cons** include a longer travel time.

## Which should you choose
Choose the AREX Express if you need to reach Seoul Station quickly and comfortably. Opt for the all-stop train if you are looking to save money or if your destination is near one of the stops along the route.

> **Good to know:** The all-stop train includes key stops like Incheon International Airport Terminal 1 and 2, Unseo, and Cheongna International City, among others.
