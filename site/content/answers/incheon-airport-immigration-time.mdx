## When you need it
Immigration processing times at Incheon Airport vary significantly based on the time of day and the season. **Peak hours can see waits of 60 to 90+ minutes**, especially during afternoons, weekends, and holidays. Off-peak hours, such as early mornings, typically have shorter waits ranging from 15 to 50 minutes.

> Good to know: Arriving early in the morning can significantly reduce your wait time.

## How to use it
The immigration process at Incheon Airport involves several steps:
- **Arrival**: Follow signs to the immigration area.
- **Prepare Documents**: Have your passport, visa (if required), and arrival card ready.
- **Queue**: Join the appropriate line.
- **Immigration Check**: Present documents; may include fingerprinting and photos.
- **Clearance**: Receive passport stamp and proceed to baggage claim.

## Cost
Expedited immigration options are available for those eligible or willing to pay. **SES auto-gates are free after enrollment** and offer the fastest processing. For a paid option, the Fast Track service costs approximately ₩27,000 per person, providing priority lanes for security and departure immigration.

> Tip: Register for SES auto-gates for the quickest immigration processing.
